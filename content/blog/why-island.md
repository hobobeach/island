## Introduction

[Island](https://island0.com) is a human-only online community. The aim of Island is to facilitate discourse between real people by doing our best to ensure that the community is made up of only real people.

In 2025, bots accounted for over 53 percent of all traffic on the Internet according to the [2026 Thales Bad Bot Report](https://cpl.thalesgroup.com/resources/application-security/2026-bad-bot-report), and over the past 12 months, AI-driven bot activity has increased more than tenfold. The rise of Large Language Models (LLMs) has turned social media platforms into "conduits for AI-driven content, prioritizing consumption over authentic social engagement" (https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00146-023-01857-0).

At the same time, the dominant platforms have little to no incentive to thwart synthetic engagement. A [2024 study by 5th Column AI](https://web.archive.org/web/20240804201809/https://5thcolumn.ai/blog/elon-was-right-about-bots/?ref=internet2-0.com) analyzed over 1.2 million X accounts and estimated that 64% might be bots. Twitter's famous "Blue Checkmark" was once a mechanism for authenticating users but now has become something that anyone has the ability to pay several dollars a month for now under Elon Musk's ownership of the platform. And the traditional metrics of success (active users, number of posts and comments, likes and upvotes, etc.) have always been fundamentally misaligned with the prevention of bot activity.

## The General Rules

1. **An account must belong to a human being.** They cannot be created by or for an AI or a bot. The goal is to keep the community free from automated traffic and content - AI agents, bots, etc. We do not make distinctions between "good" and "bad" bots, Island does not permit any at all.

1. Accounts cannot be used by AI or bots (of any type).

2. No AI generated content is allowed. This means currently:

- AI cannot make new discussion posts
- AI cannot comment

# Detection

Any traffic found to be automated is banned. Any account found to be using AI to generate content is banned. We err on the side of false positive. Unfortunately, we can predict there will be real people banned.
